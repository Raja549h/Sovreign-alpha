merkle chain for tamper evidence, provides independent verification capability.

# SECTION 4: CRYPTOGRAPHIC AUDITABILITY

## 4.1 Proof Certificate Architecture

Each recommendation generates a cryptographic audit certificate containing:
SHA-256 proof hash, server-generated immutable timestamp, plain English
reasoning, quantitative confidence score, and agent lineage.

## 4.2 Merkle Chain

Certificates are linked in a merkle chain providing tamper evidence. Any
modification breaks the chain, ensuring integrity, sequence preservation,
and completeness verification.
