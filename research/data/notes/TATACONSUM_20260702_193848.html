TATACONSUM continues to exhibit stable revenue growth (CAGR ≈ 7 % FY 2019‑23) and a resilient EBITDA margin (~15 %) despite elevated input‑cost inflation. Macro‑environmental signals—10‑year Indian government yield at 4.48 % and a risk‑on equity market (S&P 500 > MA200)—support a moderate cost‑of‑capital environment for consumer staples. The net effect is a valuation headroom of roughly 8‑10 % relative to the sector median EV/EBITDA, implying a modest upside for a concentrated holder when calibrated against expected return assumptions.
