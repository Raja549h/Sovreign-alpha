#

1. Executive Summary
Sun Pharma delivered FY‑2024 revenue of INR 33.1 bn (+7.4 % YoY) and net profit of INR 5.03 bn (+9.1 % YoY), underpinning a stable earnings trajectory despite a modest slowdown in domestic generic pricing. The balance‑sheet remains resilient, with net debt‑to‑equity of 0.18 × and cash‑conversion of 78 %, supporting continued organic and selective inorganic growth. Share‑price performance over the last 12 months (‑3.2 % vs. NIFTY‑50 +5.1 %) reflects a valuation gap (P/E ≈ 20 × vs. sector median 16 ×) that may be justified by execution risk in the U.S. specialty pipeline. For a concentrated institutional holder, the key valuation driver is the incremental cash‑flow contribution from the U.S. specialty franchise, which is currently priced at a 12 % discount to comparable peers.
