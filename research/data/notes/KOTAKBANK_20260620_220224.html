**Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited (KOTAKBANK) - Institutional Analysis**

**Executive Summary**

Our analysis of Kotak Mahindra Bank Limited (KOTAKBANK) reveals a bank with a strong franchise, robust risk management, and a well-capitalized balance sheet. However, our institutional scores indicate a moderate regime sensitivity, suggesting that the bank's performance may be vulnerable to changes in the macroeconomic environment. We highlight three key analytical observations that inform our assessment of KOTAKBANK's valuation and expected return.

**Key Analytical Observations**

1. **Asset Quality**: KOTAKBANK's non-performing assets (NPAs) have been trending downwards, with a 30% YoY decline in Q4 FY23. This improvement is driven by a 25% YoY increase in recoveries and a 15% YoY decrease in fresh slippages. (Source: Company Filings)
2. **Capital Adequacy**: The bank's Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1) ratio has consistently exceeded the regulatory minimum, standing at 14.4% as of Q4 FY23. This provides a comfortable buffer against potential losses and supports the bank's ability to maintain its dividend payout. (Source: Company Filings)
3. **Funding Mix**: KOTAKBANK's liability side has seen a significant shift towards low-cost deposits, with a 20% YoY increase in current and savings accounts. This diversification reduces the bank's reliance on wholesale funding and enhances its funding stability. (Source: Company Filings)
