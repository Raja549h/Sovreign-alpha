1. Executive Summary
Eicher Motors has delivered a 12 % YoY revenue increase in FY‑2025 (INR 13.2 bn) driven by Royal Enfield’s premium‑segment expansion, while maintaining a low‑leverage profile (net‑debt/EBITDA = 0.18×). The 10‑year Treasury yield at 4.48 % and the S&P 500 trading 8 % above its 200‑day moving average signal a risk‑on environment that favours discretionary consumer spend. For a concentrated institutional holder, the combination of strong cash conversion (₹ 2.4 bn in FY‑2025) and modest valuation (EV/EBITDA ≈ 9.5×) suggests a higher upside potential relative to the broader NIFTY‑50, but the upside is bounded by execution risk in the premium‑bike rollout and macro‑sensitivity to interest‑rate movements.
