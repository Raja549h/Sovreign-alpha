Research Methodology

Sovereign Alpha Autonomous Framework

The 4D Scoring System

Our autonomous pipeline uses a rigorous 4D scoring mechanism to filter signals before they are logged as predictions:

  • Technical Structure (D1): Evaluation of price action, moving averages, relative strength, and volume context.
  • Institutional Positioning (D2): Analysis of FII/DII flow changes, options open interest, and delivery volume.
  • Macro Regime Context (D3): Classification of the broader market environment (e.g., Risk-On, Risk-Off) and its effect on the asset.
  • Fundamental / Catalyst (D4): Assessment of recent earnings surprises, corporate actions, or sector momentum.

Only assets scoring high conviction across multiple dimensions proceed to the risk manager.

The Veto Mechanism

Once a high-conviction signal is generated, it faces our automated Risk Governance (Veto) Engine.

The Veto Engine strictly checks for conditions such as extreme over-extension, opposing institutional flows, or adverse macro regimes. If a risk threshold is breached, the signal is Vetoed (Risk-Rejected) and logged in the Veto Archive to track capital preservation and avoided drawdowns.

The Transparent Ledger

The core ethos of Sovereign Alpha is extreme intellectual honesty and transparency.

Every cleared prediction is immutable. It is logged with a cryptographic hash and placed into the Prediction Ledger. We actively track the outcome of these predictions as HIT or MISS.

Self-Auditing Rule: A MISS is never deleted. Every failed prediction receives a post-mortem note and remains on the public ledger.
